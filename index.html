Created by a Reader, For Readers

Hi, I'm Carrie, a lifelong bookworm. I love quirky, funny and heartwarming fiction, romance, romantasy, historical fiction, celebrity memoirs, poetry, and books about road trips or art heists.

I created this app because I wanted a better way to manage my TBR than 190 open Safari tabs on my phone. I also love talking with friends about books but often forget titles just after finishing a book!

Nightstand is a simple tool for tracking your TBR and finished books. You can easily move a book from your TBR to finished shelf when you are done and give it a rating and review.

Here's where Nightstand is extra simple and unique - this isn't a social network, no friends to manage, no other reviews to wade through. This is your reading life, just for you!

There are two sharing features - You can send a book recommendation to a friend in a text or iMessage and you can create an image with a book cover, title, author and your short review and rating to post on Instagram, TikTok, etc.

Nightstand also includes import features (move those Goodreads books right on over!) and export tools.
